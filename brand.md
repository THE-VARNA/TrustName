# Brand - TrustName

Status: active

TrustName uses a premium glass interface with cool charcoal foundations, cyan verification accents, signal green success states, amber review warnings, and coral denial states. The UI should feel like an identity control room for serious protocol teams: transparent, evidence-rich, and calm under risk.

## Palette

- Background: deep graphite / ink
- Glass surfaces: translucent blue-black with white hairline borders
- Primary: verification cyan
- Success: signal green
- Warning: amber
- Critical: coral
- Data accents: teal, blue, lime, and rose used sparingly

## Typography

- Sans: Geist
- Mono: Geist Mono for wallet addresses, hashes, scores, and evidence IDs

## Voice

Concise, evidence-first, and judge-demo friendly. Avoid vague claims. Prefer "Fresh primary domain verified" over "User looks trusted".
